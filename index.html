Short Bio

I am currently a PhD candidate at the School of Computer Science and Engineering, University of New South Wales Sydney, Australia]. My supervisors are Dr. Lina Yao and Dr. Sen Wang. I am an Australian Government Research Training Program (RTP) scholarship holder between 2017-2020 (awarded in 2016).

Before joining UNSW in 2017, I worked as a Research Assistant at The University of British Columnbia, Canada and a Digital Integerated Circuit Design Engineer at Spreadtrum Communications, Inc, China, respectively during 2015-2017. I received my Master degree from University of Chinese Academy of Sciences in 2015, and my Bachelor Degree from Jilin University in 2012, both major in Microelectronics.

My current research interest lies in solving key open problems in biological and medical fields through building computational models, developing machine-learning techniques, and designing effective and efficient algorithms.
